Yodle CMS3

CMS3 is a microsite publisher optimized for scale, controlled customizability, and AB Testing. This has occupied the all of my recent time at Yodle (and much more of my life outside of work than I care to admit).

Built for Scale

  • Powers over 11,000 clients in hundreds of industries.
  • Mass updates allow nimble fixes and new features even at scale. No one is left in the dust!
  • Integrates with Yodle's

Built for (Controlled) Customization

  • Modular design system along 4 dimensions: layout, style, font, and color.
  • Hundreds of possible combinations for a site's look and feel.
  • Clients can customize logo, photos, and content.
  • Clearly defined methods of customization mean the state of any site is always clear and queryable.

Built for Testing

  • Lightweight AB testing via Optimizely
  • Sites can easily be republished with a new feature or theme for more robust AB testing.
  • Optimizations can be easily rolled out after successful tests.
  • Aggregated analytics across all of CMS3 means smaller sites can still benefit from testing despite lower traffic.
